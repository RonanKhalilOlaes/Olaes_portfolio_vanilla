TimeTogether

Background & Summary

TimeTogether is a cutting-edge calendar app designed to simplify scheduling by integrating advanced AI and smart automation features. Developed to cater to busy individuals who juggle multiple responsibilities, TimeTogether allows users to optimize their time without the stress of manually managing appointments and events. By leveraging machine learning and user preferences, the app learns your habits, preferences, and typical schedules, making it capable of automatically suggesting the best times for meetings, appointments, and personal activities.


Challenges

Key features of TimeTogether include:

  1. Scheduling: The app automatically suggests the best available times for appointments based on past behavior and preferences. One-Tap Scheduling: Users can confirm their schedule with just one tap, eliminating the hassle of manual entry. Seamless Integration: TimeTogether integrates with other calendar and communication tools for real-time updates. Personalized Recommendations: The app learns over time to offer increasingly accurate scheduling suggestions, considering user priorities and preferences. Collaboration: TimeTogether helps coordinate meetings with others, offering time slots that work for all participants without the need for endless email exchanges.

Solution

To tackle these challenges, we developed WhatSupper, an AI-powered app designed to save both money and time. By simply taking a picture of a grocery flyer, users can instantly access tailored recipe suggestions based on discounted ingredients. The app leverages AI to analyze deals, recommend budget-friendly meals, and streamline meal planning for ultimate convenience and efficiency.


User Flow

With WhatSupper, we aimed to create an intuitive user experience that streamlines grocery shopping and meal planning.
The app’s core functionality—scanning flyers, generating recipes, and personalizing ingredient choices—guides users through a simple yet effective user flow.

  • Home Screen: Users can either take a picture of a flyer or browse existing deals.
  • Smart Flyer Scan & Crop: The app detects and allows cropping of the flyer for precise deal extraction.
  • Recipe Generation: AI suggests recipes based on scanned deals, user preferences, and available pantry items.
  • Personalization: Users can modify ingredients, swap items, and adjust meal recommendations.
  • Explore Recipes: Users can browse by cuisine, meal type, or cooking style for variety.
  • Save & Shop: Users can save recipes, create shopping lists, and optimize their grocery trips.

    Design Process

    Our design process prioritized precision, personalization, and variety to ensure the app meets diverse user needs.

  • Smart Flyer Scanning & Cropping – Users can take a picture of a grocery flyer, and for greater accuracy, the app includes a cropping feature to refine the selection of items to be analyzed. This ensures precise recognition of ingredients and deals.

  • AI-Powered Recipe Generation – Once the flyer is scanned, AI suggests meal ideas based on available discounts, ensuring users make the most of their budget. The generated recipes consider affordability, nutrition, and ingredient availability.

  • Personalized Ingredient Selection – Users can customize their ingredient preferences, dietary restrictions, and available pantry items to receive tailored recipe recommendations that suit their tastes and needs

  • Recipe Browsing by Cuisine & Style – To enhance variety, the app allows users to explore different meal options across multiple cuisines and cooking styles. Whether they’re looking for quick meals, cultural dishes, or healthy alternatives, WhatSupper provides inspiration beyond just budget-friendly recipes.
